Embeddersembedder.py + embedders/

BaseEmbedder defines the contract; concrete backends plug in. OpenAICompatibleEmbedder talks to any /v1/embeddings endpoint, and TokenChunkingEmbedder adds token-aware chunking & batching for OpenAIEmbedder and Qwen3Embedder.

Selection & Chunkingselector.py + chunker.py

FileFilter selects source files by language preset / glob / .kbignore; Chunker splits their content into token-bounded Chunk objects (Markdown-aware via LangChain splitters).

Configurationconfig.py

ConfigFactory loads YAML from configs/ (env vars override) into typed dataclasses. Three trees: embedding, storage, vector-store. The backend / model properties resolve the active variant.
